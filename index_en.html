Application Guidelines

For “Dealer” (Exhibitors)

Apply now

You can download a template for an illustration for the catalog from here.
* Either a PNG format or a PSD format. Do not change the size of an illustration. The file size must be less than 10 MB.
* You don't have to zip it. Send it to us via the application form as an attachment.

We're recruiting the “Dealer” (exhibitors), from regardless of whether they are individuals or legal entities, who design, build, or distribute input devices like keyboards or the parts of them.

We're going to inform all the participants (including the Dealers) of Do's and Don'ts by the organizers and the venue through e-mail and our social media at a later date. The Dealers will also receive in the same way the detailed instructions for setting up their booth in the venue.
In addition, the information is to be described in the catalogs which will be distributed later, doubling as admission passes.

We'd like you to disable anti-spam filter for info@kb-summit.com

We'll update later about the information of the bank.

Start Date March 01, 2020 00:00 (JST)
Application Deadline April 06, 2020 00:00 (JST)
Requirements You must be at least 15 years of age, and must not be a student in a junior high school.
You must complete your application through the form by the application deadline.
You must submit an illustration for the catalog, with the format and the size we specified.
You must complete all your payment.
* We're sorry but we are unable to refund any fees, once an application is completed.
Number of Spaces Available We're going to offer a total of 24 spaces regardless of how many Dealers are accepted.
In case we receive a larger number of applications for the spaces than available, you may be rejected or be asked to reduce your requested spaces as a result of consideration.
We kindly ask for your understanding.
Basic Space With a table of width 1800 mm and depth 450 mm, 2 chairs, and 3 priority admission passes for the Dealer.
Booth Cost 7000 yen
Extra Spaces 6000 yen / space (with 2 chairs and 2 priority admission passes for the Dealer)

* In case we receive a larger number of applications for the spaces than available, you may be rejected or be asked to reduce your requested spaces after consideration with the information of your application.
* You mustn't use outlet boxes in the venue. Use your own portable battery charger if you need.

General Participation

当イベントでは、自作キーボードを自由に並べられ、キーボードの撮影もできる「展示交流スペース」を設営予定です。
当日の入場待ち列が解消後、あるいは状況を見てスペースの設営をして公開開始を行います。場所および開始時のアナウンスは会場内において主催またはスタッフより行います。
皆様がお持ちのキーボードなど持ち寄りふるってご参加ください。
また、展示スペースのみ電源を開放いたします。お持ちのキーボードをかっこよく光らせましょう。

商品撮影などにも可能な簡易撮影セットも一角に設置いたします。ご自身のお持ちのキーボードを設置、撮影することが可能です。
